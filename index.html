Founders Resilience

Who we are

Startup founders, employees—really, attempting-world changers of any stripe—seeking reminders, methods and encouragement to maintain a healthy and happy mind.

Why I started this

After what felt like a flood of coming-out stories about founders' mental health battles, particularly with depression, it seemed clear many struggle in silence balancing the startup lifestyle's pressures.

Human needs, like being able to relax and be vulnerable, get neglected; the natural product of an enviroment loud on achievement while silent on struggle. We seek to redress that balance, becoming stronger—and most importantly, happier—in the process.

What we aim to provide

A space to recuperate, untangle personal challenges and grow with other creative folk. Sharing the unique challenges of the startup lifestyle and foster a more healthy relationship with ambition!
